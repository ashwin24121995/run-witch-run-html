Game Features

Everything That Makes
Run Witch Run Amazing

Discover all the features packed into this endless runner — from fast-paced action to beautiful pixel art and cross-platform support.

⚡

Fast-Paced Action

The game speed increases progressively as you run further. Every second demands sharper reflexes and quicker decisions. No two runs feel the same — the challenge grows with you.

❤️

Health Management

Your witch starts with 3 hearts. Collect health pickups scattered throughout the run to restore HP. Taking damage from enemies or obstacles reduces your health — manage it wisely to survive longer.

🏆

Score Tracking

Every meter you run adds to your score. Collect bonus items for multipliers. Your best run is saved locally — challenge yourself to beat your personal record every session.

🎨

Beautiful Pixel Art

Hand-crafted pixel art sprites and backgrounds bring the magical night sky world to life. Smooth 60fps animations ensure a visually stunning experience on every device.

📱

Cross-Platform Play

Built with HTML5 and WebGL — run it in any modern browser on desktop, tablet, or mobile. Full keyboard support for desktop and optimized touch controls for mobile devices.

🔊

Sound & Music

Immersive background music sets the magical atmosphere. Crisp sound effects for jumps, power-ups, and collisions make every action feel satisfying. Toggle sound on/off anytime.

🌙

Magical Night World

Fly through a beautifully designed night sky filled with stars, clouds, and mystical elements. The world scrolls endlessly with dynamic backgrounds that change as you progress.

💫

Power-Ups

Collect special power-ups that grant temporary abilities — invincibility shields, speed boosts, and score multipliers. Time your power-up usage for maximum effect.

🚀

No Install Required

100% browser-based. No app store, no download, no account needed. Open the page and start playing instantly. Works offline once loaded thanks to service worker caching.

Designed for Every Device

Whether you're on a desktop with a keyboard or a phone with touch controls, Run Witch Run adapts perfectly. The responsive layout ensures the game fills your screen optimally on any device.

  • Full keyboard support — Arrow Keys, WASD, Space
  • Touch-optimized controls for mobile and tablet
  • Fullscreen mode for immersive gameplay
  • Works on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
  • Offline play after first load
