💡 Tips & Tricks

  • Collect hearts to restore health and survive longer.
  • Speed increases over time — stay focused!
  • Jump over low obstacles, duck under high ones.
  • Power-ups give temporary invincibility — use them wisely.
  • On mobile, tap left/right sides of the screen to move.
  • Try to keep a rhythm — panicking leads to mistakes.

📱 Mobile Controls

  • Tap the left side of the screen to move left.
  • Tap the right side to move right.
  • Tap the center or swipe up to jump.
  • Swipe down to slide under obstacles.
  • Use the buttons below the game for easy control.
