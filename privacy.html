Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Welcome to Run Witch Run. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit and use our website and game.

1. Information We Collect

Run Witch Run is a free browser-based game. We collect minimal information to provide and improve our service:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous data about how you interact with the website, such as pages visited and time spent.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device type, operating system, and screen resolution for compatibility purposes.
  • Local Storage: Your game progress and high scores are stored locally in your browser — this data never leaves your device.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us via our contact form, we collect your name and email address solely to respond to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the game and website.
  • To improve and optimize the gaming experience.
  • To respond to your questions and support requests.
  • To monitor website performance and fix technical issues.
  • To understand how players interact with the game.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your game progress and high scores locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers. We may use minimal session cookies for website functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

4. Third-Party Services

Run Witch Run does not integrate third-party advertising networks or sell your data to third parties. The game is self-hosted and does not rely on external analytics services that track personal information.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect. Since most game data is stored locally in your browser, you maintain control over it. You can clear your browser's local storage at any time to remove saved game data.

6. Children's Privacy

Run Witch Run is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of any communications from us.
  • Clear your local game data by clearing your browser's local storage.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through our Contact page.
